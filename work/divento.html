• iPhone application for Divento

January 2012 - Mars 2012

W

hile I was doing my master's degree at Télécom Paristech a friend and me decided to take on a free-lance project for an on-line ticketing service called Divento©. The idea was to create an iPhone application where users could view different tours of the city and if they chose to, they could buy tickets offered by this service.

This application offered different services such as viewing offline maps (helpful when users don't have an international data plan) and the possibility of tracing the path of the tours in the map. This way users could have a way to travel around the London area and follow the course of their tour, where in each place they could choose to buy tickets.
